About Blood Donation

Why Donate Blood?

  • Blood donation saves lives every day.
  • It is used in surgeries, trauma care, and cancer treatments.
  • A single donation can help multiple patients.

Who Can Donate?

  • Weight: At least 50 kg (110 lbs)
  • Good health condition
